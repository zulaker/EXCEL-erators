About Us

The Mzammomtsha Primary School was established in 2010 in the semi township in Drift Sands.The school started of as a primary school at first and has now evolved into a high school for learners from the first grade right through to matric.

Vision

The school's vison is to produce litterate students who are educated and independant in mind to make sound decisions and choices that will pave a successful journey into their early adulthood lives.

Mission

The school's mission is to shape and groom its students into well educated leaders and examples of the future.It unlocks the full potential of every single child as the generation that lives of its right to education regardless of status in society.The school strives to prioritize meeting the educational needs of each student to the best of its abilities.

Goals

The school's goal and purpope of having this online presence is to engage with the public more effectively,keep the guardians and the public up to date on significant events,activities,achievements and most importantly live by transperant standards that Mzamomtsha Primary School stands for.
